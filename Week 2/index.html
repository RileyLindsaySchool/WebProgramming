Nacho Libre

Nacho Libre is a family-friendly film filled with comedy around every turn. It contains themes of following your dreams, giving to the poor, and struggles with pride.

Characters

  • Ignacio (Nacho)
  • Stephen (Esqueleto)
  • Sister Encarnacion
  • Ramses

About Movie

Nacho Libre is about a friar named Ignacio who works at a catholic church / orphanage in Oaxaca, Mexico. Ignacio dreams of becoming a wrestler, under the pseudonym "El Nacho", but must keep his dreams a secret, or he will get kicked out of the monastery. Ignacio teams up with a local homeless man named Stephen, and together they hope to make a better life for themselves and the orphans by becoming the greatest wrestlers in all of Mexico.
